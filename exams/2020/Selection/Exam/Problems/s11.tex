\en{Let $a_0,a_1,a_2,\ldots$ be an infinite sequence of non-negative integers satisfying $a_i \leq i$ for every $i \geq 0$ and such that for every integer $n\geq 1$
\[
\binom{n}{a_0} + \binom{n}{a_1} + \dots + \binom{n}{a_n} = 2^n. 
\]
Prove that each non-negative integer appears in the sequence.
