In the acute triangle \(ABC\), let \(M\) be the midpoint of the altitude \(h_b\) through \(B\) and let \(N\) be the midpoint of the altitude \(h_c\) through \(C\). Further, let \(P\) be the intersection of \(AM\) and \(h_c\) and \(Q\) the intersection of \(AN\) and \(h_b\). Show that \(M, N, P\) and \(Q\) lie on a circle.
