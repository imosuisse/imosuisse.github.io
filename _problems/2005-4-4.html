--- year: 2005 round: 4 problem: 4 ---

Let \(k_1\) and \(k_2\) be two circles touching each other externally at point \(P\). Let a third circle \(k\) touch \(k_1\) in \(B\) and \(k_2\) in \(C\) so that \(k_1\) and \(k_2\) lie inside \(k\). Let \(A\) be one of the intersection points of \(k\) with the common tangent of \(k_1\) and \(k_2\) through \(P\). The lines \(AB\) and \(AC\) intersect \(k_1\) and \(k_2\) again in \(R\) and \(S\) respectively. Show that \(RS\) is a common tangent of \(k_1\) and \(k_2\).
