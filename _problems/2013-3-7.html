Let \(O\) be the center of the circumcircle of the triangle \(ABC\) with \(AB\neq AC\). Furthermore, let \(S\) and \(T\) be points on the rays \(AB\) and \(AC\) respectively, such that \(\angle ASO=\angle ACO\) and \(\angle ATO= \angle ABO\). Show that \(ST\) bisects the segment \(BC\).
