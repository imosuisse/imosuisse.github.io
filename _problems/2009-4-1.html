Let \(GERMANYISHOT\) be a regular dodecagon. The lines \(GN\) and \(MI\) intersect at point \(P\). Prove that

  1. the circumcircle of triangle \(GIP\) is the same size as the circumcircle of \(GERMANYISHOT\).

  2. the distance \(PA\) is the same length as one side of \(GERMANYISHOT\).
