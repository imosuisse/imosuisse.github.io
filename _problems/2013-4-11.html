Let \(ABC\) be a triangle with \(\angle ACB \geq 90^\circ\) and let \(k\) be the circle with diameter \(AB\) and midpoint \(O\). The inscribed circle of \(ABC\) touches \(AC\) and \(BC\) in \(M\) and \(N\) respectively. The line \(MN\) intersects \(k\) at the points \(X\) and \(Y\). Show that \(\angle XOY = \angle ACB\).
