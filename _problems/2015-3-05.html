--- year: 2015 round: Final Round number: 5 topic: Combinatorics ---

Let \(m\) be a positive integer. On the MO blackboard, the number \(1\) is written \(2^m\) times. In a step, one chooses two numbers \(a\) and \(b\) on the blackboard and replaces them each by \(a+b\). Show that after \(m2^{m-1}\) steps the sum of the numbers is at least \(4^m\).
