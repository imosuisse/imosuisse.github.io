Let \(ABC\) be a triangle with \(AC>BC\). Its incircle touches sides \(BC\), \(CA\) and \(AB\) at \(D\), \(E\) and \(F\), respectively. Let \(P\) be the point on segment \(AC\) such that \(BP\parallel DE\). Let \(\Omega\) be the circumcircle of triangle \(AFD\). Line \(EF\) intersects \(\Omega\) again at \(Q\) and line \(PQ\) intersects \(\Omega\) again at \(R\). Prove that \(PEBR\) is a cyclic quadrilateral.
