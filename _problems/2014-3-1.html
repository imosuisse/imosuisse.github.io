--- year: 2014 round: 3 problem: 1 ---

The points \(A, B, C\) and \(D\) lie in this order on the circle \(k\). Let \(t\) be the tangent to \(k\) through \(C\) and \(s\) the reflection of \(AB\) at \(AC\). Let \(G\) be the intersection of the lines \(AC\) and \(BD\) and \(H\) the intersection of the lines \(s\) and \(CD\). Show that \(GH\) is parallel to \(t\).
