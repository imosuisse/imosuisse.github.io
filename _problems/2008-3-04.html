Consider three faces of an \(n\times n \times n\) cube that meet at one of the corners of the cube. For which \(n\) is it possible to cover them completely and without overlapping with strips of paper of size \(3\times 1\)?

Remark: The paper strips can also be glued over the edges between these cube faces.
