--- year: 2014 round: Selection number: 11 topic: Number Theory source: Bulgaria TST 2004 ---

Determine all natural numbers \(n\) with the following property: For any prime \(p<n\), \(n-\lfloor \frac{n}{p}\rfloor p\) is not divisible by the square of a positive integer greater than \(1\). Remark: For \(x \in \mathbb{R}\), \(\lfloor x\rfloor\) is defined as the largest integer less than or equal to \(x\).
