--- year: 2006 round: Selection number: 2 topic: Combinatorics ---

Let \(n\geq 5\) be an integer. Determine the largest integer \(k\) such that a polygon with \(n\) vertices and exactly \(k\) interior \(90^\circ\) angles exists?

Remark: The polygon does not have to be convex, but the edge must not intersect itself.
