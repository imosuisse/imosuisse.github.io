Let \(ABC\) be a triangle with \(AB=AC\neq BC\) and let \(I\) be the center of its inscribed circle. The line \(BI\) intersects \(AC\) in \(D\), and the perpendicular to \(AC\) though \(D\) intersects \(AI\) in \(E\). Show that the reflection of \(I\) over \(AC\) is on the circumcircle of \(BDE\).
