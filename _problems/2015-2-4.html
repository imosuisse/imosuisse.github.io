Let \(ABC\) be an acute triangle and \(W\) the intersection of the angle bisector of \(\angle ACB\) and the side \(AB\). Furthermore, let \(I_A\) and \(I_B\) be the incenters of the triangles \(AWC\) and \(WBC\) respectively. The lines \(I_AW\) and \(I_BB\) intersect at point \(D\). Let \(M\) be the midpoint of the line \(DI_B\). Show that \(MWBC\) is a cyclic quadrilateral.

Note: The three angle bisectors of a triangle intersect at a point, the centre of the inscribed circle.
