--- year: 2005 round: Second Round number: 4 topic: Geometry ---

Let \(ABC\) be a triangle with \(\angle BAC = 60^\circ\). Find all points \(P\) in the interior of this triangle with the following property: If \(D\) is the projection of \(P\) onto the line \(BC\), \(E\) is the projection of \(P\) onto \(CA\) and \(F\) is the projection of \(P\) onto \(AB\), then \(\angle EDF = 30^\circ\).
