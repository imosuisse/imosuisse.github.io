Let \(ABCD\) be a square and \(M\) a point on the segment \(BC\). The angle bisector of the angle \(\angle BAM\) intersects the line \(BC\) at point \(E\). Furthermore, the angle bisector of the angle \(\angle MAD\) intersects the line \(CD\) at the point \(F\). Show that \(AM\) and \(EF\) are perpendicular to each other.
