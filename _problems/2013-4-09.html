--- year: 2013 round: Selection number: 9 topic: Algebra ---

Find all functions \(f\colon\mathbb{R}_{>0} \times \mathbb{R}_{>0} \to \mathbb{R}_{>0}\) such that the following two conditions are satisfied:

  • \(f\left(f\left( \frac{x}{y},y\right),z\right)=x \cdot f(1,z)\) for all \(x,y,z \in \mathbb{R}_{>0}\);

  • The function \(g:\mathbb{R}_{>0} \to \mathbb{R}_{>0}\), which is defined by \(g(x)=f(x,x)\)

for all \(x \in \mathbb{R}_{>0}\), is monotonic.
