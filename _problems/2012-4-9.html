Determine the largest natural number \(k\) with the following property: The set of natural numbers can be divided into \(k\) disjoint subsets \(A_1,\dots,A_k\) such that each natural number \(n\ge 15\) can be written as a sum of two different elements from \(A_i\) for each \(i \in \{1,\dots,k\}\).
