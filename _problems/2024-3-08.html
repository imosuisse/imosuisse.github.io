Let \(ABCD\) be a cyclic quadrilateral with \(\angle BAD<\angle ADC\). Let \(M\) be the midpoint of the arc \(CD\) not containing \(A\). Suppose there is a point \(P\) inside \(ABCD\) such that \(\angle ADB=\angle CPD\) and \(\angle ADP=\angle PCB\). Prove that the lines \(AD\), \(PM\), \(BC\) are concurrent.
