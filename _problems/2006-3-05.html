A circle \(k_1\) is contained in another circle \(k_2\) touching it in the point \(A\). A line passing through \(A\) intersects \(k_1\) again in \(B\) and \(k_2\) in \(C\). The tangent to \(k_1\) at \(B\) intersects \(k_2\) in the points \(D\) and \(E\). The tangents to \(k_1\) through \(C\) touch \(k_1\) in the points \(F\) and \(G\). Prove that \(D,E,F\) and \(G\) lie on a circle.
