Let \(A, B, C, D, E\) be five points lying on a circle in this order, such that \(AE\) is a diameter and \(BC > CD\). Let \(F\) be the intersection of \(AD\) and \(BE\). Let \(O\) be the circumcenter of the triangle \(BFD\). Let the reflection of the line \(BD\) across \(BC\) and the reflection of the line \(BD\) across \(DC\) intersect at \(Y\).

Prove that \(\angle OYC = 90^\circ\).
