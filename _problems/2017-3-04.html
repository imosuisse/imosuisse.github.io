Let \(n\) be a positive integer and let \(p\) and \(q\) be prime numbers such that \[\begin{aligned} pq &\mid n^p + 2,\\ n + 2 &\mid n^p + q^p. \end{aligned}\] Show that there are infinitely many positive integers \(m\) with \(q \mid 4^m n +2\).
