--- year: 2003 round: 3 number: '01' ---

\(67\) students write an exam. The exam consists of \(6\) multiple-choice questions, all of which must be answered with “yes” or “no”. Each student answers all \(6\) questions. A correct answer to the \(k\)th question gives \(k\) points, an incorrect answer \(-k\) points.

  1. Show that at least two students have completed the exam sheet in the same way.

  2. Show that at least four students scored the same number of points.
