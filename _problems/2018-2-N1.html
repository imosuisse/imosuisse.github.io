Let \(n\geq 2\) be a positive integer. Let \(d_1, \ldots, d_r\) be all the positive divisors of \(n\) that are smaller that \(n\). Determine every \(n\) for which \[\operatorname{lcm}(d_1, \ldots, d_r) \neq n.\] Remark: For \(n=18\) we have for instance \(d_1 = 1, d_2 = 2, d_3 = 3, d_4 = 6, d_5=9\) and thus \(\operatorname{lcm}(1,2,3,6,9) = 18\).
