--- year: 2004 round: Final Round number: 8 topic: Number Theory ---

A list of positive integers is displayed on a blackboard. The following operation is now performed repeatedly: Select any two numbers \(a,b\), wipe them out and write \(\gcd(a,b)\) and \(\operatorname{lcm}(a,b)\) in their place. Show that the content of the list remains constant after a certain point in time.
