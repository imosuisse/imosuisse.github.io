--- year: 2018 round: 4 number: '03' ---

Along the coast of a round island there are 20 different villages. Each of these villages has 20 fighters and all 400 fighters have different strengths. Every pair of neighbouring villages \(A\) and \(B\) organises a competition during which all \(20\) fighters from village \(A\) battles individually against every fighter from the village \(B\). The winner of a battle is always the stronger fighter. We say that the village \(A\) is stronger than the village \(B\), if during at least \(k\) out of the 400 battles, a fighter from village \(A\) has won. It turns out that every village is stronger than its clockwise neighbouring village. Determine the maximal value of \(k\) that allows such an outcome.
