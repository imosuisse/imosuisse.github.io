Let \(ABC\) be an equilateral triangle and \(P\) a point in its interior. The lines \(AP\),\(BP\) and \(CP\) intersect the sides \(BC\), \(CA\) and \(AB\) at the points \(X\), \(Y\) and \(Z\). Prove that the following applies \[|XY|\cdot |YZ| \cdot |ZX| \geq |XB|\cdot |YC| \cdot |ZA|.\]
