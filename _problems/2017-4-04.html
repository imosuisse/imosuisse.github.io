--- year: 2017 round: Selection number: 4 topic: Geometry ---

Let \(k\) be a circle and let \(AB\) be a chord of \(k\) not containing the center of \(k\). Let \(C\) be a point on \(k\), different from \(A\) and \(B\). For each choice of \(C\), let \(P_C\) and \(Q_C\) be the projections of \(A\) onto \(BC\) and \(B\) onto \(AC\) respectively. Further, let \(O_C\) be the circumcircle of \(P_{C}Q_{C}C\). Show that there is a circle \(\omega\) such that \(O_C\) lies on \(\omega\) for any choice of \(C\).
