The village of Roche has \(2020\) residents. One day, the famous mathematician Georges de Rham makes the following observations:

  • Every villager knows someone else with the same age as them.

  • For any group of \(192\) people in the village, there are always at least three of them that have the same age.

Prove that there must exist a group of \(22\) villagers that all have the same age.
