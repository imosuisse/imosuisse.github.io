Let \(A\) and \(B\) be the intersection points of two circles \(k\) and \(l\) with centre \(K\) and \(L\) respectively. Let \(M\) and \(N\) be the intersection points of \(k\) and \(l\) respectively with a line through \(A\) such that \(A\) lies between \(M\) and \(N\). Let \(D\) be the intersection of the lines \(MK\) and \(NL\). Show that the points \(M,N,B\) and \(D\) lie on a circle.
