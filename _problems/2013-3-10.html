Let \(ABCD\) be a quadrilateral containing an incircle with \(BC>BA\). Let the point \(P\) lie on the line \(BC\) such that \(BP=BA\). Show that the angle bisector of \(\angle BCD\), the perpendicular to \(BC\) through \(P\) and the perpendicular to \(BD\) through \(A\) intersect at a point.
