Let \(A_{1}A_{2}A_{3}\) be a triangle and \(\omega_1\) a circle that passes through \(A_1\) and \(A_2\). Assume that there are circles \(\omega_2, \ldots, \omega_7\) with the following properties:

  • \(\omega_k\) passes through the points \(A_k\) and \(A_{k+1}\) for \(k=2,3, \ldots, 7\) where \(A_i=A_{i+3}\)

  • \(\omega_k\) and \(\omega_{k+1}\) touch each other externally for \(k=1,2, \ldots, 6\).

Show: \(\omega_1=\omega_7\).
