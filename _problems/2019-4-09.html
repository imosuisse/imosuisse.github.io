Let \(ABC\) be an acute triangle with \(AB<AC\). Let \(E\) and \(F\) be the feet of the altitudes from \(B\) and \(C\), respectively, and \(M\) the midpoint of the segment \(BC\). The tangent line to the circumcircle of \(ABC\) at \(A\) intersects the line \(BC\) at \(P\). The line parallel to \(BC\) through \(A\) intersects the line \(EF\) at \(Q\). Prove that the line \(PQ\) is perpendicular to the line \(AM\).
