Let \(a\) and \(b\) be integers with \(a > b\geq 5\). Prove that there exist a positive integer \(k\) and positive integers \(c_1, c_2, \ldots, c_k\) with \(c_1 = a\) and \(c_k = b\), such that \(c_i^2 + c_{i+1}^2\) is a perfect square for all \(1 \leq i < k\).
