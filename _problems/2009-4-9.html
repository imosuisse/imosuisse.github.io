In an acute triangle \(ABC\), let \(BE\) and \(CF\) be altitudes. Two circles through \(A\) and \(F\) touch the line \(BC\) at \(P\) and \(Q\), such that \(B\) lies between \(C\) and \(Q\). Prove that the lines \(PE\) and \(QF\) intersect on the circumcircle of \(AEF\).
