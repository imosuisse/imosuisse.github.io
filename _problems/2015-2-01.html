On SMO Street there are \(n\) houses with house numbers \(1\) to \(n\), where \(n\) is a positive integer. The houses with odd numbers are on the left-hand side of the street, the houses with even numbers on the right. The postman Quirin wants to deliver a newspaper to each house. How many ways does he have to do this if he wants to change sides of the street after every newspaper he delivers?
