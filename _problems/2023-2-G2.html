Let \(\omega_1\) be a circle with diameter \(JK\). Let \(t\) be the tangent to \(\omega_1\) at \(J\) and let \(U \neq J\) be another point on \(t\). Let \(\omega_2\) be the smaller circle centred at \(U\) that touches \(\omega_1\) at one single point \(Y\). Let \(I\) be the second intersection of \(JK\) with the circumcircle of triangle \(JYU\) and let \(F\) be the second intersection of \(KY\) with \(\omega_2\). Show that \(FUJI\) is a rectangle.
