Let \(ABC\) be a triangle where \(\angle BAC = 90^{\circ}\), with circumcenter \(O\) and incenter \(I\). The angle bisector of \(\angle BAC\) intersects the circumcircle of \(ABC\) in \(A\) and \(P\). Let \(Q\) be the projection of \(P\) onto \(AB\), and \(R\) be the projection of \(I\) onto \(PQ\). Prove that \(RO\) bisects \(CI\).
