Let \(ABC\) be a triangle with incenter \(I\), and let \(J\) be the reflection of \(I\) with respect to line \(BC\). Let \(K\) be the second intersection of line \(BC\) with the circumcircle of triangle \(CIJ\), and \(L\) be the second intersection of line \(BI\) with the circumcircle of triangle \(AIK\). Prove that the lines \(BC\) and \(JL\) are parallel.
