Let \(ABC\) be an acute triangle with a orthocenter \(H\). A line through \(H\) intersects \(AB\) and \(AC\) at the points \(D\) and \(E\) respectively, so that \(|AD|=|AE|\). The angle bisector of \(\angle BAC\) intersects the circumcircle of \(ADE\) at the point \(K \neq A\). Show that \(HK\) bisects the line \(BC\).
