The points \(X,Y,Z\) lie in this order on a line with \(|XY| \neq |YZ|\). Let \(k_1\) and \(k_2\) be the circles with diameter \(XY\) and \(YZ\) respectively. The points \(A_1\) and \(B_1\) respectively \(A_2\) and \(B_2\) lie on \(k_1\) and \(k_2\) respectively, such that \[\angle A_1YA_2 = \angle B_1YB_2 = 90^\circ\] holds. Show that the two lines \(A_1A_2\) and \(B_1B_2\) intersect on \(XY\).
