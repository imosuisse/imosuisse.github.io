Let \(ABCD\) be a parallelogram. Assume that there exists a point \(P\) inside the parallelogram that lies on the perpendicular bisector of \(AB\) and such that \(\angle PBA=\angle ADP\). Show that \(\angle CPD=2 \,\angle BAP\) holds.
