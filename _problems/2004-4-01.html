Let \(S\) be the set of all \(n\)-tuples \((X_1,\ldots,X_n)\), where \(X_1,\ldots,X_n\) are subsets of \(\{1,2,\ldots,1000\}\), not all of which have to be different, and which can also be empty. For \(a = (X_1,\ldots,X_n) \in S\) denote \[E(a) = \text{ number of elements of } X_1 \cup \ldots \cup X_n.\] Find an explicit expression for the sum \[\sum_{a \in S} E(a).\]
