Let \(k\) be a circle and \(A\), \(B\) be two points on \(k\). At these points the tangents to \(k\) are drawn and the tangent segments \(AP\) and \(BQ\) of the same length are plotted in clockwise direction. Prove that the segment \(PQ\) is bisected by the line \(AB\).
