--- year: 2007 round: 4 problem: 6 ---

Let \(a,b,c\) be positive real numbers with \(a+b+c \geq abc\). Prove that at least two of the following three inequalities are correct: \[\frac{2}{a}+\frac{3}{b}+\frac{6}{c} \geq 6, \qquad \frac{2}{b}+\frac{3}{c}+\frac{6}{a} \geq 6, \qquad \frac{2}{c}+\frac{3}{a}+\frac{6}{b} \geq 6.\]
