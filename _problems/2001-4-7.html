Let \(ABC\) be an acute triangle with circumcircle \(O\). Let \(S\) be the circumcircle of \(A,B\) and \(O\). The lines \(AC\) and \(BC\) intersect \(S\) a second time at the points \(P\) and \(Q\) respectively. Show that the line \(CO\) is perpendicular to the line \(PQ\).
