Let points \(P\) and \(Q\) lie on a circle \(k_1\) with centre \(O\). Let \(k_2\) be the circle which is centered at \(P\) and passes through \(Q\). Define \(X\) as the second intersection of \(k_2\) and line \(PQ\), and \(Y\) as the second intersection of \(k_2\) and \(k_1\). Let \(Z\) be the intersection of \(OX\) with \(QY\). Prove that if \(PZYX\) is cyclic, then \(PYX\) is an equilateral triangle.
