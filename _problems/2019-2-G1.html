Let \(k\) be a circle with center \(O\) and let \(A\), \(B\) and \(C\) be three points on \(k\) with \(\angle ABC > 90^\circ\). The angle bisector of \(\angle AOB\) intersects the circumcircle of triangle \(BOC\) again at \(D\). Prove that \(D\) lies on the line \(AC\).
