Let \(ABC\) be an acute-angled triangle and the points \(D,E\) and \(F\) be the feet of the altitudes through \(A,B\) and \(C\) respectively. Let \(S\) be the point of intersection of the line \(EF\) with the line perpendicular to \(AC\) through \(D\). Prove that the triangle \(DES\) is isosceles.
