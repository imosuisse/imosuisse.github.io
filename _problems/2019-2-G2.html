Let \(k_1\) be a circle and \(l\) a line that intersects \(k_1\) in two distinct points \(A\) and \(B\). Let \(k_2\) be another circle outside of \(k_1\) that touches \(k_1\) at \(C\) and \(l\) at \(D\). Let \(T\) be the second intersection of \(k_1\) and the line \(CD\). Prove that \(AT=TB\).
