--- year: 2000 round: 4 problem: 15 ---

Let \(S=\{P_1,P_2,\ldots,P_{2000}\}\) be a set of \(2000\) points inside a circle of radius \(1\) where one of the points is the centre of the circle. For \(i=1,2,\ldots,2000\) let \(x_i\) denote the distance from \(P_i\) to the nearest point \(P_j \neq P_i\) in \(S\). Show that \[x_1^2+x_2^2+\ldots+x_{2000}^2 < 9.\]
