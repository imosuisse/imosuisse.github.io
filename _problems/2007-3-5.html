Determine all functions \(f\colon\mathbb{R}_{\geq 0} \rightarrow \mathbb{R}_{\geq 0}\) with the following properties:

  • \(f(1)=0\),

  • \(f(x)>0\) for all \(x>1\),

  • For all \(x,y \geq 0\) with \(x+y>0\) the following holds \[f(xf(y))f(y)=f\left(\frac{xy}{x+y}\right).\]
