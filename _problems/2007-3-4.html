Let \(ABC\) be an acute triangle with \(AB>AC\) and orthocenter \(H\). Let \(D\) be the foot of the altitude of \(A\) on \(BC\). Let \(E\) be the reflection of \(C\) at \(D\). The lines \(AE\) and \(BH\) intersect at the point \(S\). Let \(N\) be the midpoint of \(AE\) and let \(M\) be the midpoint of \(BH\). Prove that \(MN\) is perpendicular to \(DS\).
