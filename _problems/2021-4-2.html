For each prime \(p\), somewhere in the multiverse there exists a kingdom consisting of \(p\) islands numbered from \(1\) to \(p\) with a bridge between any pair of them. When Jana visits a kingdom, coronavirus restrictions mean she must obey the following rule: Directly after visiting island \(m\), she can only cross over to island \(n\) if \[p \div (m^2-n+1)(n^2-m+1).\] Show that there are infinitely many kingdoms such that Jana cannot travel to every island in this manner.
