Let \(ABC\) be an acute triangle with orthocenter \(H\) and let \(M\) and \(N\) be two points on \(BC\) such that \(\overrightarrow{MN} = \overrightarrow{BC}\). Let \(P\) and \(Q\) be the projections of \(M\) and \(N\) onto \(AC\) and \(AB\) respectively. Show that \(APHQ\) is a cyclic quadrilateral.
