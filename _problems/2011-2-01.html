Let \(ABC\) be a triangle with \(\angle CAB = 90^\circ\). The point \(L\) lies on the side \(BC\). The circumcircle of the triangle \(ABL\) intersects the line \(AC\) at \(M\) and the circumcircle of the triangle \(CAL\) intersects the line \(AB\) at \(N\). Assume that \(N\) lies on the side \(AB\) and \(M\) lies on the line \(AC\). Show that \(L, M\) and \(N\) lie on a line.
