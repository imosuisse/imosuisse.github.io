A floor plan is given which is composed of \(n\) unit squares. Albert and Berta want to cover this floor with tiles, whereby all tiles have the shape of a \(1\times 2\) domino or a T-tetromino. Albert only has tiles of one colour available, whereas Berta has dominoes in two colours and tetrominoes in four colours. Albert can cover this floor plan with tiles in \(a\) ways, Berta in \(b\) ways. Assuming that \(a\neq 0\) applies, determine the ratio \(b/a\).
