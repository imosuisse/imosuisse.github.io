Let \(n\) be a positive integer. Roger has a \((2n+1) \times (2n+1)\) square garden. He puts down fences to divide his garden into rectangular plots. He wants to end up with exactly two horizontal \(k \times 1\) plots and exactly two vertical \(1 \times k\) plots for each even integer \(k\) between \(1\) and \(2n+1\), as well as a single \(1 \times 1\) square plot. How many different ways are there for Roger to do this?
