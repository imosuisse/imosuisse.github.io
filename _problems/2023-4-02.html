Let \(S\) be a non-empty set of positive integers such that for any \(n\in S\), all positive divisors of \(2^n+1\) are also in \(S\). Prove that \(S\) contains an integer of the form \[(p_1 p_2 \dots p_{2023})^{2023},\] where \(p_1,p_2, \ldots, p_{2023}\) are distinct prime numbers, all greater than \(2023\).
