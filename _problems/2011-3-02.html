Let \(ABC\) be an acute triangle. Let \(D\), \(E\) and \(F\) be points on \(BC\), \(CA\) and \(AB\) respectively, such that \[\angle AFE=\angle BFD,\ \ \angle BDF=\angle CDE,\ \ \angle CED=\angle AEF\] Show that \(D\), \(E\) and \(F\) are the feet of the altitudes.
