--- year: 2012 round: Selection number: 12 topic: Number Theory ---

Find all integers \(m,n \geq 2\) that fulfill the following two conditions:

  • \(m+1\) is a prime number of the form \(4k + 3\) for an integer \(k\).

  • There exists a prime number \(p\) and a non-negative integer \(a\) with \[\frac{m^{2^n-1}-1}{m-1} = m^n + p^a.\]
