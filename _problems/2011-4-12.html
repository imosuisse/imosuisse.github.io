--- year: 2011 round: 4 problem: 12 ---

Let \(a > 1\) be a natural number and let \(f\) and \(g\) be polynomials with integer coefficients. Suppose there is a natural number \(n_0\) such that \(g(n) >0\) for all \(n \geq n_0\) and \[f(n)\mid a^{g(n)} - 1 \quad \text{for all} \ n \geq n_0.\] Show that \(f\) must be constant.
