Let \(ABCD\) be a cyclic quadrilateral with circumcircle \(k\). Let \(S\) be the intersection of \(AB\) and \(CD\) and let \(T\) be the intersection of the tangents to \(k\) in \(A\) and \(C\). Show that \(ADTS\) is a cyclic quadrilateral if and only if \(BD\) bisects the line \(AC\).
