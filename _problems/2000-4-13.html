The incircle of the triangle \(ABC\) touches the sides \(AB\), \(BC\) and \(CA\) at the points \(D\), \(E\) and \(F\). Let \(P\) be a point in the interior of \(ABC\) such that the incircle of \(ABP\) also touches the side \(AB\) at \(D\). Additionally, let the incircle touch the sides \(AP\) and \(BP\) at the points \(Q\) and \(R\). Show that the four points \(E, F, R\) and \(Q\) lie on a circle.
