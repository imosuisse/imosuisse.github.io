--- year: 2011 round: Final Round number: 9 topic: Number Theory ---

Let \(n\) be a positive integer. Let \(f(n)\) be the number of divisors of \(n\) that end with the digit \(1\) or \(9\) and let \(g(n)\) be the number of divisors of \(n\) that end with the digit \(3\) or \(7\). Show that \(f(n) \geq g(n)\) for all \(n \in \mathbb{N}\).
