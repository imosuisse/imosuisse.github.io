--- year: 2012 round: Selection number: 2 topic: Number Theory ---

An integer \(m\) is a true power if there are positive integers \(a\) and \(n\) such that \(n>1\) and \(m=a^n\).

  1. Show that there are \(2012\) distinct positive integers such that no non-empty subset of them sums to a real power.

  2. Show that there are \(2012\) distinct positive integers such that every non-empty subset of them sums to a real power.
