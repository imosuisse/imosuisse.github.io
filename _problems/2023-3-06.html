--- year: 2023 round: Final Round number: 6 topic: Number Theory ---

Determine all integers \(n\geq 3\) such that \[n!\mid \prod_{\substack{p<q\leq n\\ p,q \text{ prime}}}(p+q).\] Remark: The expression on the right-hand side denotes the product over all sums of two distinct primes less than or equal to \(n\). For \(n = 6\), this is equal to \((2+3)(2+5)(3+5)\).
