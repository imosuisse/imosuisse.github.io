--- year: 2016 round: Selection number: 11 topic: Number Theory author: Robert Meier ---

Let \(m\) and \(n\) be positive integers with \(m>n\). Define \[x_k = \frac{m+k}{n+k}\text{ for }k=1, \ldots, n+1.\] Show that if all \(x_i\) are integers, then \(x_1\cdot x_2 \cdot \ldots \cdot x_{n+1} - 1\) is not a power of two.
