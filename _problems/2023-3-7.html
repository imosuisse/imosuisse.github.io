In the acute triangle \(ABC\), the point \(F\) is the foot of the altitude from \(A\), and \(P\) is a point on the segment \(AF\). The lines through \(P\) parallel to \(AC\) and \(AB\) meet \(BC\) at \(D\) and \(E\) respectively. Points \(X \neq A\) and \(Y \neq A\) lie on the circumcircles of triangles \(ABD\) and \(ACE\) respectively, such that \(DA=DX\) and \(EA=EY\). Prove that \(BCXY\) is a cyclic quadrilateral.
