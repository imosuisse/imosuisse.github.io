Let \(g\) be a line in the plane. The circles \(k_1\) and \(k_2\) lie on the same side of \(g\) and touch \(g\) at the points \(A\) and \(B\) respectively. Another circle \(k_3\) touches \(k_1\) at \(D\) and \(k_2\) at \(C\). Prove that:

  1. The quadrilateral \(ABCD\) is a cyclic.

  2. The lines \(BC\) and \(AD\) intersect on \(k_3\).
