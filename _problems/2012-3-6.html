Let \(ABCD\) be a parallelogram with at least one angle not equal to \(90^{\circ}\) and \(k\) be the circumcircle of the triangle \(ABC\). Let \(E\) be the diametrically opposite point of \(B\) on \(k\). Show that the circumcircle of the triangle \(ADE\) and \(k\) have the same radius.
