Let \(A\), \(B\), \(C\) and \(D\) be four points on a circle in this order. Assume that there is a point \(K\) on the segment \(AB\) such that \(BD\) bisects \(KC\) and \(AC\) bisects \(KD\). Determine the minimal value that \(\left|\frac{AB}{CD}\right|\) can take.
