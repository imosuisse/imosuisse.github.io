Let \(ABCD\) be a rectangle with \(|AD| \leq |AB|\). Let \(M\) be the midpoint of the line \(AD\) and \(N\) the midpoint of the line \(BC\). Let the point \(E\) be the projection of \(B\) onto the line \(CM\).

  1. Show that \(ANEM\) is an isosceles trapezoid.

  2. Show that the area of the quadrilateral \(ABNE\) is half the area of \(ABCD\).
