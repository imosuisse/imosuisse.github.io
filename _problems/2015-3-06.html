--- year: 2015 round: Final Round number: 6 topic: Combinatorics ---

Consider a \(8 \times 8\) board. An inner edge is an edge that separates two \(1 \times 1\) squares. We cut the board into multiple \(1 \times 2\) domino stones. For an inner edge \(k\), denote by \(N(k)\) the number of possible ways to cut the board such that we cut along \(k\). Determine the last digit of the sum we obtain when adding the \(N(k)\) for all inner edges \(k\).
