Let \(ABCD\) be a cyclic quadrilateral with \(CD<AD\) and \(CD<BC\). The diagonals \(AC\) and \(BD\) intersect at the point \(S\). Let the reflection of the line \(AB\) at \(AC\) be \(e\) and the reflection of the line \(AB\) at \(BD\) be \(f\). The line \(CD\) intersects \(e\) and \(f\) at the points \(E\) and \(F\) respectively. Prove that the triangle \(SEF\) is isosceles.
