A palindrome is a natural number, such that in the decimal system its value is the same, regardless of whether it is read from the front or from the back (for example \(1129211\) or \(7337\)). Determine all the pairs \((m,n)\) of natural numbers such that \[(\underbrace{11\dots 11 }_{m})\cdot (\underbrace{11\dots 11}_{n})\] is a palindrome.
