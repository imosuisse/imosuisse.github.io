Let \(ABC\) be an acute-angled triangle with circumcentre \(O\). The perpendicular of \(A\) to \(BC\) intersects the circumcircle at point \(D \neq A\), and the line \(BO\) intersects the circumcircle at point \(E \neq B\). Show that \(ABC\) and \(BDCE\) have the same area.
