--- year: 2015 round: 4 number: '07' ---

Find all non-empty finite sets \(A\) of functions \(f\colon\mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{R}\) which fulfil the following property: For all \(f_1, f_2 \in A\) there exists a function \(g \in A\) such that for all \(x,y \in \mathbb{R}\): \[f_1(f_2(y)-x) + 2x = g(x+y).\]
