Consider an \(n\times n\)-matrix with the entry \(i+j-1\) in the field of the \(i\)-th row and \(j\)-th column. What is the smallest possible product of \(n\) numbers of this matrix if it is required that each row and each column contains one of these \(n\) numbers?
