Three circles of the same size \(k_1,k_2,k_3\) intersect non-tangentially at a point \(P\). Let \(A\) and \(B\) be the centres of the circles \(k_1\) and \(k_2\). Let \(D\) and \(C\) be the point of intersection of \(k_3\) with \(k_1\) and \(k_2\), respectively, which is different from \(P\). Show that \(ABCD\) is a parallelogram.
