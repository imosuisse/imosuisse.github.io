Let \(ABCD\) be a trapezoid with \(AB \parallel CD\) and \(AB > CD\). The points \(K\) and \(L\) lie on the sides \(AB\) and \(CD\) respectively with \(AK/KB = DL/LC\). The points \(P\) and \(Q\) lie on the line \(KL\) such that \[\angle APB = \angle BCD \qquad \text{and} \qquad \angle CQD = \angle ABC.\] Show that the points \(P,Q,B\) and \(C\) lie on a circle.
