Let \(ABC\) be a triangle, and let \(l_1\) and \(l_2\) be two parallel lines. For \(i=1, 2\), assume \(l_i\) meets the lines \(BC\), \(CA\) and \(AB\) at \(X_i\), \(Y_i\) and \(Z_i\) respectively. Suppose that the line through \(X_i\) perpendicular to \(BC\), the line through \(Y_i\) perpendicular to \(CA\), and finally the line through \(Z_i\) perpendicular to \(AB\), determine a non-degenerate triangle \(\Delta_i\). Show that the circumcircles of \(\Delta_1\) and \(\Delta_2\) are tangent to each other.
