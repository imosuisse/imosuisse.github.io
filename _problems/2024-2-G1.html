Let \(ABC\) be a triangle. The angle bisector of \(\angle ACB\) intersects \(AB\) in \(D\). Let \(T\) and \(H\) be points on the circumcircles of \(CAD\) and \(CDB\) respectively, such that \(TH\) is a common tangent to the two circumcircles and \(C\) is inside the quadrilateral \(BATH\). Show that \(BATH\) is cylic.
