Let \(f,g\) be two polynomials with integer coefficients and let \(a,b\) be integer fixed points of \(f \circ g\). Prove that integer fixed points \(c,d\) of \(g \circ f\) exist with \(a+c=b+d\). Remark: For two polynomials \(p,q\), \(p\circ q\) is defined by \((p\circ q)(x) = p(q(x))\).
