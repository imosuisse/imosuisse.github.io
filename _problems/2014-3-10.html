Let \(k\) be a circle with diameter \(AB\). Let \(C\) be a point on the line \(AB\) such that \(B\) lies between \(A\) and \(C\). Let \(T\) be a point on \(k\) such that \(CT\) is a tangent to \(k\). Let \(l\) be the parallel to \(CT\) through \(A\) and let \(D\) be the intersection of \(l\) and the perpendicular to \(AB\) through \(T\). Show that the line \(DB\) bisects the segment \(CT\).
