--- year: 2005 round: Selection number: 9 topic: Geometry ---

Let \(A_1 A_2 \ldots A_n\) be a regular \(n\)-vertices. The points \(B_1,\ldots,B_{n-1}\) are defined as follows:

  • For \(i=1\) or \(i=n-1\), \(B_i\) is the centre of the side \(A_i A_{i+1}\);

  • For \(i \neq 1, i \neq n-1\), let \(S\) be the intersection of \(A_1 A_{i+1}\) and \(A_n A_i\). The point \(B_i\) is then the intersection of the angle bisector of \(\angle A_i S A_{i+1}\) with \(A_i A_{i+1}\).

Prove that: \[\angle A_1 B_1 A_n + \angle A_1 B_2 A_n+ \ldots + \angle A_1 B_{n-1} A_n = 180^\circ.\]
