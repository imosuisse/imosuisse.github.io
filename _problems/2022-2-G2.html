Let \(k_1\) be a circle centred at \(M\) and \(\ell\) a line tangent to \(k_1\) at \(A\). Let \(k_2\) be a circle inside \(k_1\) also tangent to \(\ell\) at \(A\). Let \(P\) be a point on \(\ell\) different from \(A\). The second tangent to \(k_1\) through \(P\) touches \(k_1\) at \(T\). Let \(B\) be the second intersection of \(AT\) and \(k_2\), and let \(C\) be the second intersection of \(PB\) and \(k_2\). Show that \(ATCM\) is a cyclic quadrilateral.
