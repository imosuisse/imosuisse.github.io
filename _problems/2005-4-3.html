Let \(n \geq 1\) be a positive integer. Let a regular \(4n\)-gon of side length \(1\) be arbitrarily divided into finitely many parallelograms.

  1. Prove that at least one of the parallelograms in the decomposition is a rectangle.

  2. Determine the sum of the areas of all rectangles in the decomposition.
