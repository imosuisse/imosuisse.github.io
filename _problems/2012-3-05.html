Let \(n\) be a positive integers. Let \(A_1,A_2, \ldots, A_k\) be distinct \(3\)-element subsets of \(\left\{1, 2, \ldots, n\right\}\) such that \(|A_i\cap A_j| \neq 1\) for all \(1\leq i,j\leq k\). Determine all \(n\) for which there are \(n\) such subsets.
