--- year: 2013 round: 3 number: '01' ---

Determine all triples \((a,b,c)\) of positive integers such that the sets \[\Big\{\gcd(a,b),\ \gcd(b,c),\ \gcd(c,a),\ \operatorname{lcm}(a,b),\ \operatorname{lcm}(b,c),\ \operatorname{lcm}(c,a)\Big\}\] and \[\{2, 3, 5, 30, 60\}\] are equal.

Note: For example, the quantities \(\{1,2013\}\) and \(\{1,1,2013\}\) are equal.
