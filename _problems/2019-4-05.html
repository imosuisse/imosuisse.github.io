Let \(ABC\) be a triangle with \(AB=AC\) and let \(M\) be the midpoint of \(BC\). Let \(P\) be a point such that \(PB<PC\) and \(PA\) is parallel to \(BC\). Let \(X\) and \(Y\) be points on the line \(PB\) and \(PC\), respectively, so that \(B\) lies on the segment \(PX\), \(C\) lies on the segment \(PY\), and \(\angle PXM=\angle PYM\). Prove that the quadrilateral \(APXY\) is cyclic.
