Let \(ABC\) be a triangle with \(\angle BAC = 60^\circ\). The points \(D\) and \(E\) lie on the sides \(AC\) and \(AB\) respectively. The lines \(BD\) and \(CE\) intersect the circumcircle of \(ABC\) again at the points \(X\) and \(Y\) respectively. Let \(BD\) and \(CE\) intersect \(S\). Prove that the lines \(BY\) and \(CX\) are parallel if and only if \(AESD\) is a cyclic quadrilateral.
