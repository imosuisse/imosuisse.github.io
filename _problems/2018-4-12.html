David and Linus play the following game: David chooses a subset \(Q\) of \(\{1, \dots ,2018\}\). Then Linus chooses a natural number \(a_1\) and computes inductively the numbers \(a_2, \dots, a_{2018}\) with \(a_{n+1}\) being the product of all positive divisors of \(a_n\). Let \(P\) be the set of integers \(k\in\{1,\dots,2018\}\) for which \(a_k\) is a perfect square. Linus wins if \(P=Q\). Otherwise David wins. Determine which player has a winning strategy.
