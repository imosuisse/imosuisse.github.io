--- year: 2009 round: Selection number: 6 topic: Number Theory ---

Let \(P\) be the set of the first \(2009\) prime numbers and let \(X\) be the set of all positive integers that only have prime factors from \(P\). Determine all natural numbers \(k\) for which a function \(f\colon X \rightarrow X\) exists which fulfils the following equation for all \(m,n \in X\): \[f(mf(n))=f(m)n^k.\]
