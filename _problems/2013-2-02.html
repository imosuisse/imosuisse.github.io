Let \(M_1\) and \(M_2\) be the midpoints of the circles \(k_1\) and \(k_2\) respectively and call one of their intersections \(P\). Furthermore, \(k_1\) intersects the line \(M_1M_2\) in \(Q\). Show that the perpendicular to the line \(M_1M_2\) through the point \(M_2\) and the line \(PQ\) intersect at \(k_2\).
