Let \(n\) be an even positive integer. We partition the numbers \(1,2,\ldots,n^2\) into two sets \(A\) and \(B\) with the same size such that all of the \(n^2\) numbers belong to exactly one of the two sets. Let \(S_A\) and \(S_B\) be the sum of all the elements in \(A\) respectively \(B\). Determine all \(n\) such that there is a partition with \[\frac{S_A}{S_B} = \frac{39}{64}.\]
