The function \(f\colon[0,1]\rightarrow\mathbb{R}\) has the following properties:

  • \(f(x) \geq 0\) for all \(x \in [0,1]\)

  • \(f(1) = 1\)

  • \(f(x+y) \geq f(x)+f(y)\) for all \(x, y, x+y \in [0,1]\)

Prove: \(f(x)\leq 2x\) for all \(x \in [0,1]\)
