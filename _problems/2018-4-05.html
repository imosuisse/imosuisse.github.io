Let \(n\) be a positive integer. We consider an \(n\times n\) grid. We colour \(k\) squares in black, such that given any three columns, there exists at most one row that intersects the three columns at a black square. Prove that \[\frac{2k}{n} \leq \sqrt{8n-7}+1.\]
