Let \(O\) be the circumcenter of an acute triangle \(ABC\). The line \(OA\) intersects the altitude \(h_b\) at \(P\) and the altitude \(h_c\) at \(Q\). Let \(H\) be the orthocenter of \(ABC\). Prove that the circumcenter of \(PQH\) lies on the median of \(ABC\) through \(A\).

Remark: The altitude \(h_a\) is the line through \(A\) perpendicular to \(BC\).
