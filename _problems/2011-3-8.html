Let \(ABCD\) be a parallelogram and \(H\) the orthocenter of the triangle \(ABC\). The line parallel to \(AB\) through \(H\) intersects \(BC\) in \(P\) and \(AD\) in \(Q\). The parallel to \(BC\) through \(H\) intersects \(AB\) in \(R\) and \(CD\) in \(S\). Show that \(P\), \(Q\), \(R\) and \(S\) lie on a circle.
