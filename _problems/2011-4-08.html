--- year: 2011 round: Selection number: 8 topic: Combinatorics ---

Show that there are more than \(10^{13}\) ways to place \(81\) kings on an \(18\times 18\) chessboard in such a way that no two kings attack each other.

Remark: Two kings can attack each other if the squares on which they stand have a common side or a common corner.
