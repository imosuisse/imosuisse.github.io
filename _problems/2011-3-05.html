The tangents in \(A\) and \(B\) to the circumcircle of the triangle \(ABC\) intersect at point \(T\). The circle through the points \(A\), \(B\) and \(T\) intersects \(BC\) and \(AC\) again at \(D\) and \(E\) and \(CT\) intersects \(BE\) at \(F\). Assume that \(D\) is the midpoint of \(BC\). Calculate the ratio \(BF:FE\).
