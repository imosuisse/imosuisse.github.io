Denote by \(\mathcal{F}\) the set of all functions \(f\colon \mathbb{R}\rightarrow \mathbb{R}\) that satisfy the equation \[f(x+f(y)) = f(x)+f(y)\] for all \(x,y\in\mathbb{R}\). Determine all rational numbers \(q\) such that for every function \(f\in\mathcal{F}\), there exists some \(z\in\mathbb{R}\) with \(f(z) = qz\).
