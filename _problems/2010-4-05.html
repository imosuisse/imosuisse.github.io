Let \(P\) be a finite set of prime numbers and let \(\ell(P)\) be the largest possible number of consecutive natural numbers such that each of these numbers is divisible by at least one prime number from \(P\). Prove the inequality \(\ell(P) \geq |P|\) and show that equality holds if and only if the smallest element of \(P\) is greater than \(|P|\).
