Given is a circular bus route with \(n\geq 2\) stops, which can be travelled in both directions. The distance between two neighbouring stops is called a section. One of the stops is called Zurich. A bus should start in Zurich, then travel exactly \(n+2\) sections and end up back in Zurich. It must visit each stop at least once. It can turn round at any stop. How many possible routes are there?
