Let \(a_1,a_2,\ldots\) be an arithmetic sequence of integers. Assume that for \(1\leq k\leq 50\) each \(a_k\) is divisible by \(k\).

  1. Prove that \(a_{51}\) is divisible by \(51\) and \(a_{52}\) is divisible by \(52\).

  2. Is \(a_{53}\) always divisible by \(53\)?

Remark: The sequence \(a_1,a_2,\ldots\) is arithmetic if the difference \(a_{i+1}-a_i\) is the same for all \(i\).
