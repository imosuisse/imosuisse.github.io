--- year: 2017 round: Final Round number: 7 topic: Number Theory ---

Let \(n\) be a positive integer such that there are exactly \(2017\) different pairs of positive integers \((a,b)\) that satisfy the equation \[\frac{1}{a} + \frac{1}{b} = \frac{1}{n}.\] Prove that \(n\) is a square number.

Remark: \((7,4) \neq (4,7)\)
