Let \(n\) be a positive integer and let \(P(x)\) denote the polynomial \[x^n + a_{n-1}x^{n-1} + \dots + a_1 x + a_0\] with real coefficients and \(a_0 \neq 0\), whose \(n\) roots are all pairwise distinct, positive real numbers. Assuming that \(P(x)\) divides \(P(2x)P(x/2)\), prove that \[\frac{a_{n-1} a_1}{a_0} \ge \frac{9n^2}8.\]
