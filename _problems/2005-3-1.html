Let \(ABC\) be a triangle and let \(D, E, F\) be the midpoints of \(BC, CA, AB\) respectively. The medians \(AD\), \(BE\) and \(CF\) intersect at centroid \(S\). At least two of the quadrilaterals \[AFSE,\quad BDSF,\quad CESD\] are cyclic. Show that the triangle \(ABC\) is equilateral.
