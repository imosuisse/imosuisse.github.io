--- year: 2009 round: 4 problem: 5 ---

Let \(ABCD\) be a convex quadrilateral and let \(P\) and \(Q\) be points inside the quadrilateral \(ABCD\) such that \(PQDA\) and \(QPBC\) are cyclic quadrilaterals. Assume that a point \(E\) exists on the line \(PQ\) such that \(\angle PAE=\angle QDE\) and \(\angle PBE=\angle QCE\). Show that \(ABCD\) is a cyclic quadrilateral.
