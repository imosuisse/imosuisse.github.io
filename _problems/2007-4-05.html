Let \(n>1\) and \(m\) be natural numbers. A parliament consists of \(mn\) deputies who have formed \(2n\) commissions, so that

  • Each committee consists of \(m\) deputies.

  • Each deputy is a member of exactly \(2\) commissions.

  • Two committees have at most one member in common.

Determine the largest possible value of \(m\) as a function of \(n\) so that this is possible.
