Let \(ABC\) be an equilateral triangle and let \(D\) be a point in the interior of the side \(BC\). A circle touches \(BC\) in \(D\) and intersects the sides \(AB\) and \(AC\) in the points \(M, N\) and \(P,Q\), respectively. Prove that: \[|BD|+|AM|+|AN| = |CD|+|AP|+|AQ|.\]
