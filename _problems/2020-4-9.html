We call a set \(S\) of integers laikable if for any positive integer \(n\) and any \(a_0,a_1,\ldots,a_n\in S\), all integer roots of the polynomial \(a_nx^n+\ldots+a_1x+a_0\) are also in \(S\), given that it is not the zero polynomial. Find all laikable sets of integers that contain all numbers of the form \(2^a-2^b\) for positive integers \(a, b\).
