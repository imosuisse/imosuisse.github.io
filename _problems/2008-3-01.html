Let \(ABC\) be a triangle with \(\angle AC \neq 45^\circ\) and \(\angle ABC \neq 135^\circ\). Let \(P\) be the point on the line \(AB\) with \(\angle CPB~=~45^\circ\). Let \(O_1\) and \(O_2\) be the circumcircle centres of the triangles \(ACP\) and \(BCP\). Show that the area of the quadrilateral \(CO_1PO_2\) is the same as the area of the triangle \(ABC\).
