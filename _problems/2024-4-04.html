Let \(a_1,\dots,a_{2^{2024}}\) be a sequence of pairwise distinct positive integers. Define \[S_n = \frac{1}{1+a_1}+\frac{a_1}{(1+a_1)(1+a_2)}+\cdots +\frac{a_1a_2\cdots a_{n-1}}{(1+a_1)(1+a_2)\cdots(1+a_n)}.\] Determine how many sequences \(a_1,\dots,a_{2^{2024}}\) exist, such that \(S_{2^i} =\frac{2^i}{2^i+1}\) for all \(0\leq i\leq 2024\).
