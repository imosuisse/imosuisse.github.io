Find all pairs \((\alpha,\beta)\) of positive real numbers with the following properties:

  • For all positive real numbers \(x,y,z,w\) the following holds \[x+y^2+z^3+w^6 \geq \alpha (xyzw)^\beta.\]

  • There exists a quadruple \((x,y,z,w)\) of positive real numbers such that \[x+y^2+z^3+w^6 = \alpha (xyzw)^\beta.\]
