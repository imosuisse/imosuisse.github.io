Let \(n\) be a positive integer. Each of the numbers \(\{1,2,\ldots,n\}\) is coloured white or black. One can now repeatedly select a number and recolour it as well as all numbers that are not divisors of it. Initially all numbers are white. For which \(n\) can you achieve that all numbers are black at some point?
