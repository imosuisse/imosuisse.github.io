--- year: 2014 round: Final Round number: 4 topic: Combinatorics ---

Consider an infinitely large square grid. For which pairs \((a,b)\) can each of the squares be colored with one of \(a\cdot b\) colors, such that each rectangle of size \(a \times b\) or \(b \times a\) that is placed in the grid always contains a unit square of each color?
