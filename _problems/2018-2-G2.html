Let \(ABCD\) be an inscribed quadrilateral with circumcenter \(O\) such that the diagonals \(AC\) and \(BD\) are perpendicular. Let \(g\) be the line symmetric of the diagonal \(AC\) with respect to the angle bisector of \(\angle BAD\). Prove that \(O\) lies on the line \(g\).
