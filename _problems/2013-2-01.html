--- year: 2013 round: Second Round number: 1 topic: Combinatorics source: Putnam ---

A group of \(2013\) people sit down at a round table, evenly distributed. After they have sat down, they notice that there is a name tag at each seat and that no one has sat down at the seat with their name on it. Show that they can turn the table so that at least two people have the correct name tag in front of them.
