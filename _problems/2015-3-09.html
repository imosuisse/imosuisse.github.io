Let \(p\) be an odd prime number. Determine the number of tuples \((a_1,a_2,\dots,a_p)\) of positive integers with the following properties:

  • \(1\leq a_i\leq p\) for all \(i=1,\dots,p\)

  • \(a_1+a_2+\dots +a_p\) is not divisible by \(p\)

  • \(a_1a_2 + a_2a_3 +\dots a_{p-1}a_p + a_pa_1\) is divisible by \(p\)
