Let \(\Gamma\) be a circle and \(P\) a point outside \(\Gamma\). A tangent from \(P\) to the circle touches it at \(A\). Another straight line through \(P\) intersects \(\Gamma\) at the different points \(B\) and \(C\). The bisector of \(APB\) intersects \(AB\) at \(D\) and \(AC\) at \(E\). Prove that the triangle \(ADE\) is isosceles.
