Let \(ABC\) be a triangle with \(AB > AC\) and let \(M\) be the midpoint of the side \(AC\). Let \(D\) also be a point on the side \(AB\) such that \(DB = DC\). The line parallel to \(BC\) through \(D\) and the line \(BM\) intersect at the point \(K\). Show that \(\angle KCD = \angle DAC\) holds.
