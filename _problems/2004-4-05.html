A block consisting of \(7\) unit cubes has the shape of a \(2 \times 2 \times 2\) cube with a missing corner unit cube. An arbitrary unit cube is removed from a cube of edge length \(2^n\), \(n \geq 2\). Show that the remaining solid can always be constructed from blocks.
