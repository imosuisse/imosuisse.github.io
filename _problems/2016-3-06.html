Let \(a_n\) be a sequence of positive integers defined by \(a_1 = m\) and \(a_n = a^2_{n-1}+1\) for \(n>1\). We call a pair \((a_k, a_l)\) interesting if

  • \(0<l-k<2016\), and

  • \(a_k\) divides \(a_l\).

Show that an \(m\) exists such that the sequence \(a_n\) does not contain an interesting pair.
