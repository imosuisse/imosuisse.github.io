For an integer \(a\geq 2\), denote by \(\delta(a)\) the second largest divisor of \(a\). Let \((a_n)_{n\geq 1}\) be a sequence of integers such that \(a_1\geq 2\) and \[a_{n+1} = a_n + \delta(a_n)\] for all \(n\geq 1\). Prove that there exists a positive integer \(k\) such that \(a_k\) is divisible by \(3^{2022}\).
