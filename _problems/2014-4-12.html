Let \(n\) be a positive integer and \(a_1,a_2, \dots,a_n\) also be positive integers. We periodically extend the sequence such that \(a_{n+i} = a_i\) for all \(i\geq 1\). Assume that the following two conditions hold:

  • \(a_1\leq a_2\leq\cdots \leq a_n\leq a_1 +n\).

  • \(a_{a_i} \leq n+i-1\) for \(i=1,2\dots,n\).

Show that: \[a_1+a_2+dots +a_n \leq n^2\]
