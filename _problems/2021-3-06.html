Let \(\mathbb{N}\) be the set of positive integers. Let \(f\colon\mathbb{N}\to\mathbb{N}\) be a function such that for every \(n\in\mathbb{N}\) \[f(n)-n < 2021 \quad \text {and} \quad \underbrace{f(f(\cdots f(f}_{f(n)}(n))\cdots)) = n.\] Prove that \(f(n) = n\) for infinitely many \(n\in\mathbb{N}\).
