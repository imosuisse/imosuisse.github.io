Let \(ABCD\) be a parallelogram. The points \(P\) and \(Q\) lie inside \(ABCD\) on the diagonal \(AC\), where \(|AP|=|CQ|<|AC|/2\). The line \(BP\) intersects \(AD\) at point \(E\), the line \(BQ\) intersects \(CD\) at \(F\). Show that \(EF\) is parallel to the diagonal \(AC\).
