--- year: 2025 round: Selection number: 6 topic: Number Theory ---

Let \(a_1, a_2, a_3, \dots\) be an infinite sequence of real numbers satisfying, for all integers \(n \ge 1\), \[a_{\lfloor \frac{n}{1}\rfloor} \cdot a_{\lfloor \frac{n}{2}\rfloor} \cdot \ldots \cdot a_{\lfloor \frac{n}{n}\rfloor}= 2^{n^2}.\] Prove that \(\frac{a_{n+1}-a_n}{n+1}\) is an integer for all \(n \geq 1\).
