Let \(O\) be an interior point of an acute triangle \(ABC\). Let \(A_1\), \(B_1\) and \(C_1\) be the projections of \(O\) onto the sides \(BC\), \(AC\) and \(AB\). Let \(P\) be the intersection of the perpendiculars to \(B_1C_1\) and \(A_1C_1\) through the points \(A\) and \(B\) respectively. Let \(H\) be the projection of \(P\) onto \(AB\). Show that the points \(A_1,B_1, C_1\) and \(H\) lie on a circle.
